P-graph Research Award

The International Institute for P‑graph (IIPG) is pleased to announce the P‑graph Research Award. It recognizes outstanding Master’s or PhD dissertations that make exceptional, original contributions to P‑graph theory, methods, or applications. The award celebrates early‑career researchers whose work demonstrates high quality, strong impact, and significant advancement of P‑graph research.

Elegibility

You can apply for the P-graph Research Award if:

  • You have completed an original Master’s or PhD dissertation (single‑author, final submission) at a certified university between 1 May 2021 and 30 April 2026.
  • Your dissertation has not been submitted for this award before.
  • You have an active IIPG membership at the time of submission. If you are not a member yet, you can register here
  • You provide an executive summary. Dissertations in languages other than English are acceptable; however, the required executive summary must be in English.
  • Your dissertation shows substantial and significant use or application of P‑graph concepts, methods, or tools.

Submission

Materials described in the official poster must be submitted in PDF format by 30th June 2026 to secretary@p-graph.org.

Evaluation and Award

Submissions will be evaluated by an IIPG review panel on originality, technical quality, significance of P‑graph contribution, and potential impact or applicability. The recipient will receive a certificate and a prize of €500.

Questions

For inquiries, contact secretary@p-graph.org.
